Population by age group

According to the 2015 Census, the age group with the highest population in Southern Leyte is 10 to 14, with 45,160 individuals. Conversely, the age group with the lowest population is 80 and over, with 5,680 individuals.


Historical population

The population of Southern Leyte grew from 72,369 in 1903 to 429,573 in 2020, an increase of 357,204 people over the course of 117 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a positive growth rate of 0.39%, or an increase of 7,823 people, from the previous population of 421,750 in 2015.


The total number of registered voters or electorate in Southern Leyte for the year 2019 was 292,337.
